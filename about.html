About

Practical tool guidance for small online operators.

RichAssets is being built as a straightforward resource for comparing software, AI tools, and digital systems used by small businesses, side-hustle builders, and independent operators.

The site focuses on practical fit: what a tool helps with, who it suits, what to watch out for, and whether it makes sense before a business has a full team.

What RichAssets avoids

  • Guaranteed income claims
  • Hidden affiliate relationships
  • Overcomplicated tool stacks
  • Recommendations that pretend every business needs the same setup

How recommendations are handled

RichAssets may use affiliate links where available. Affiliate relationships do not change the price paid by the reader, and they should be disclosed clearly before or near relevant links.
